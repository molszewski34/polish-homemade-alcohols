Sea ​​buckthorn tincture

It's one of my best drinks i made 2 years ago. At first i wasn't convince, because many people on youtube said this tincture is one of "healing" ones and when someone describe tincture this way it mostly means she taste bad but i was so wrong about this one.

Picking fruits

Sea buckthorn fruits are one one of the most annoying fruits to pick. The main reason is a structure of fruit. Berry of sea buckthorn is almost completly fill with juice, so every atempt can end with squizing the juice and waste it in result. At the top of that every little branch has thorns so picking it without plan is a good way to waste a day and pick only half of what you tried to get.

Luckly people found better way to pick sea buckthorn. You pick small branches with fruits and freeze them for few hours until they are are rock solid. Normaly you would also want to freeze fruits before puting them into jar because fruits need to be frozen anyway. This way fruits loose strange taste and become lightly sour and sweet. So every time i go to pick some sea buckthorn i take scisors and garden shears. I cut mostly good branches. Good branches should look like those on the picture.

Preparing fruits to next stage

Then i cut branches into pieces so more can fit into freezer. After few hours or next day they are ready to tear apart. Even then it is not so easy and i need to pay attention to thorns and if they are still solid.

Final Stage

You can make as much as you want but proportions are always the same

On every 0.5 kg of sea buckthorn you need:

0.5 kg of sugar

750 ml 50% alcohol.

I use 90% alcohol and dilute it half to half with water.

Put fruits and sugar in the jar and pour alcohol. Mix it up and put a jar in some dark place. Wait 5 weeks and remember to shake it atleast once per week. Chance to get mold is rather low but not low enough to put away and forget about it.

This is how it looks after 3 weeks of maceration. Notice how much dust is flowing inside container.

After 5 weeks remove the fruits with strainer and filter it. I filter it in few stages. Pour the liquid over paper towel. Second is coffee filter. Just take a funnel, put a filration material inside and pour liquid.

After filtration, tincture should mature for 6 months. During this time it will darken and get new flavors. At the end it should have dark amber color.
